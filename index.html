About Our Temple

Welcome to Karandayil Sreebhadra Bhagavathi Temple, a sacred destination nestled in the spiritually rich land of Pandalam, Pathanamthitta, Kerala. Our temple stands as a beacon of divine grace and traditional Kerala architecture.

Dedicated to Sreebhadra Bhagavathi, the temple attracts devotees from far and wide seeking blessings, peace, and spiritual enlightenment. The serene atmosphere and magnificent surroundings create a perfect environment for prayer and meditation.

With a rich history and deep cultural significance, our temple continues to preserve and promote the ancient traditions and rituals that have been passed down through generations. Whether you seek spiritual solace or wish to witness the architectural beauty, Karandayil Sreebhadra Bhagavathi Temple welcomes all with open arms.

"Where devotion meets divinity in the land of Lord Ayyappa"
